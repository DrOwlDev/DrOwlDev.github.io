Little Learners Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 14, 2026  |  Publisher: Dr. Quiz Learning Center

This Privacy Policy describes how Dr. Quiz Learning Center ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use Kids Toddler & Preschool Games — Little Learners ("the app"), our Android educational game application for young children published on Google Play (package: dev.drowl.app5). We are committed to protecting children's privacy and complying with Google Play Families policies, COPPA, and applicable data protection laws.

1. Information We Collect

Little Learners does not collect, store, or transmit personal information to our servers. The app is designed for offline play with no account required. Game progress (such as earned stars) is stored locally on your device only using Android's private app storage. We do not collect names, email addresses, photos, location data, contacts, or any other personally identifiable information.

2. No Advertising or Analytics

Little Learners contains no third-party advertising and no analytics or tracking SDKs. We do not use Google AdMob, Firebase Analytics, or similar services. Your child's play activity is not monitored, profiled, or shared with advertisers.

3. On-Device Features

The app uses only on-device capabilities to deliver educational games:

  • Text-to-speech: Spoken prompts are generated on your device; no audio is sent to our servers.
  • Sound effects: Short in-app sounds are played locally.
  • Local storage: Stars and preferences are saved on your device and can be cleared from the app menu.

4. Parent-Only Links

Links to our privacy policy, Google Play listing, and developer website are available only through the parent-facing overflow menu and require confirmation before opening in your device's browser. Child-facing game screens do not contain external links, social features, or in-app purchases.

5. Permissions

Little Learners requests only the permissions necessary for core functionality. The app does not request access to the camera, microphone, contacts, calendar, or location. An internet connection is not required for gameplay.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Little Learners is directed at children under 13, particularly toddlers and preschoolers (ages 2–5). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because we do not collect personal data, parental consent for data collection is not required. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our app, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

All game progress is stored locally on your device. You can reset progress at any time using Menu → Clear Progress in the app. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

8. Data Security

Because data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers, the security of that information depends on your device's settings and protections. We recommend keeping your device software up to date and using a screen lock if the device is shared.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the latest revision. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our practices, contact us at dr.owl.dev@gmail.com.
